History of Swimming

Swimming originated from Britain in 1830 and was first apart of the Olympic games in 1896
In 1908, the world swimming association, Federation Internationale de Natation (FINA), was formed.
FINA is a well-known swimming brand that is known for "approving" swimsuits, such as kneeskins that has a tag on the back that says "FINA approved" with a qr code.
